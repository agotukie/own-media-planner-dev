  • how to plan actions
    ▪ move items from the menu to the calendar
    ▪ click the purple button to calculate the reach
    ▪ blue bars show the level of reach/awareness
    ▪ to test different scenarios you can move items within the calendar
    ▪ to recalculate reach press the purple button again
  • how it works
    ▪ each action/touchpoint generates some awareness
    ▪ each action has some potential, some make more impression than others
    ▪ you can only get limited reach with your media, therefore for example stacking all actions in one day is not effective
    ▪ you get better results planning your action through more extended period
  • difference between action types
    ▪ regural - low but long term effect, everyday simple communication
    ▪ middle - average effect
    ▪ super - hight effect but not long lasting - for super communitation/promotions, events that occure once-twice a year
    different touchpoints generate different engagement, to simply estimation this planner assumes there are three different types
  • disclaimer
    ▪ we don't have control over social media algorithm
    ▪ a lot depends on you actual post (is it intesting, visually atractive, etc.)
    ▪ you social media and your followers are unique to you
    ▪ you reach depends on number of platforms that you use and cross usage of platforms by followers
    ▪ not all touchpoints are applicable to you communication
  • why to use?
    ▪ to stay consistend/motivated to post
    ▪ to consider new touchpoints
